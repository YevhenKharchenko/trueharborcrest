About the Game

You know those bubble shooter games from way back?

We basically took that same addictive gameplay and optimized it for mobile. Players aim carefully, bounce shots off walls, and match colors to clear clusters of bubbles. The further you get, the trickier it becomes - you'll need to really plan your moves and make every shot count.

Gameplay Overview

Planning ahead is key to clearing the board efficiently.

  • Shooting Mechanics

    Shoot bubbles at matching colors - get three or more together and they'll pop, giving you room to work with.

  • Strategy & Angles

    Sometimes you've gotta get creative - bank shots off the walls, weird angles, whatever it takes to nail those impossible clusters.

Key Features

  • Classic bubble shooter gameplay

  • Increasingly complex level layouts

  • Simple and intuitive controls

  • Bright and colorful visual effects

  • Short, replayable sessions
